Install client

Windows

  1. Open Webions in Chrome
  2. Click on the three squares
  3. Go to "More tools" > "Create shortcut"
  4. Check "Open as window"
  5. Click "Create"
  6. Open Webions from tour desktop.

Android

  1. Open Webions in Chrome
  2. Tap the menu icon (3 dots in upper right-hand corner)
  3. Tap "Add to homescreen
  4. You can type your own name of app and finally click "Add"

IOS

  1. Open Webions in Safari
  2. Tap the Share icon (the square with an arrow pointing out of it) at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. You can type your own name of app and finally click "Add"

Controls

White targeting

In case of attack somebody - firstly you must whiteTarget him, by klicking T button (mobile) or T key on keyboard (computer). If in your area are few players, click T key multiple times to select right player or monster.

Red targeting

By clicking S key - you can change your white target into red target And only by redtarget you can hit your enemy by fist fighting (you must stand near him).

Distance shoting

D key is for distance shoting. Before you shot somebody - remember to red target him.

Healing

Your health is most important thing in Webions - to increase it - click H key. If you click heal to quick once again - you will see grey dust - You are exhousted.
